Before you buy

What to Look for in a Whey Protein

Four things that actually matter.

💪

Protein per 100g

Aim for 70g+ of protein per 100g of powder. This tells you the actual concentration — not just what the serving size shows.

🧬

Isolate vs Concentrate

Isolate is more refined — higher protein, lower fat and lactose. Better if you're lactose-sensitive or cutting. Concentrate is cheaper and works fine for bulking.

🏷️

Ingredient Label

Fewer ingredients = cleaner product. Avoid products where the protein blend lists "amino spiking" amino acids like taurine or glycine at the top.

📦

Price per Gram of Protein

Divide the total cost by total grams of protein in the pack. This is the only fair way to compare products across different sizes and brands.
