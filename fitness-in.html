Before you buy

What to Check Before Buying Home Gym Equipment

📐

Measure your space first

For a treadmill, you need at least 2m × 1m of clear floor space. For a bench, 2.5m × 1.5m. Resistance bands and a pull-up bar need practically nothing.

⚖️

Start lighter than you think

Most beginners buy weights that are too heavy and stop using them. Start with 3–5kg dumbbells. You can always go heavier once your form is solid.

🔧

Check warranty and service

For treadmills and benches, ensure at least 1-year on parts. Brands like Cockatoo and Slovic offer DIY installation videos — check before buying.

📦

Buy from Amazon Fulfilled

Always prefer "Fulfilled by Amazon" listings for returns. Large items like treadmills are harder to return from third-party sellers.
