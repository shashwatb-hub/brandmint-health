Before you buy

What to Look for in a Multivitamin

🎯

Buy formulated for your demographic

A multivitamin for men has different nutrient ratios than one for women or seniors. Never buy a generic "adults" product when gender- or age-specific formulations exist at the same price.

📊

Check % RDA on label

Look for at least 100% RDA for Vitamin C, D, B12, and zinc. Too much of fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K) can be toxic — avoid products with 300%+ of these.

🔬

Bioavailability matters

Methylcobalamin B12 absorbs better than cyanocobalamin. Magnesium glycinate absorbs better than oxide. Premium brands use the better-absorbed forms — check the ingredient label.

🍽️

Always take with food

Fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K) require dietary fat to absorb properly. Taking with breakfast or lunch — not on an empty stomach — significantly improves absorption and reduces nausea.
